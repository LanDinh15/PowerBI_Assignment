# Superstore Sales & Profit Analysis (Power BI)

## Overview
This project analyzes Superstore retail performance using **Power BI** to evaluate sales trends, profitability, regional performance, and discount impact from **2014 to 2017**.  
The goal is to support data-driven business decisions by identifying top-performing regions, loss-making areas, and year-over-year trends.

## Key Business Questions
1. Which region generated the highest total sales?
2. What was the overall trend in total sales from 2014 to 2017?
3. How did sales and profit in 2015 compare to 2017?
4. Which region had the lowest profit over time?
5. Did Superstore meet its sales target in 2015?

## Dashboard Pages

### 1. Sales Overview
- KPI cards for Total Sales, Total Profit, Profit Margin, and Order Count
- Total sales by region
- Sales trends by year, quarter, and month
- Geographic visualization of sales across U.S. states

### 2. Profit & Discount Analysis
- Profit by region and sub-category
- Comparison of total sales and total profit by year
- Discount impact analysis
- Sales target performance using KPI and gauge visuals

---

## Key Insights
- **West region** generated the highest total sales ($725K), contributing the largest share of overall revenue.
- Total sales showed a **steady upward trend** from 2014 to 2017.
- **2015 recorded the lowest sales**, while **2017 achieved the highest sales and profit**, indicating improved performance over time.
- The **Central region consistently had the lowest total profit**, suggesting margin challenges.
- Superstore **met its sales target in 2015**, despite lower overall sales compared to later years.

## Conclusion
This project demonstrates how Power BI can be used to transform raw retail data into actionable insights.  
The analysis highlights regional performance gaps, long-term sales growth, and profitability challenges that can inform pricing, discount, and regional strategies.
